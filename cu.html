A smart building is a structure that uses advanced technology to automatically control and monitor its operations, including heating, ventilation, air conditioning, lighting, security, and other systems. Smart buildings use sensors, actuators, and other devices to collect data, analyze it, and make decisions based on that data to optimize building performance, reduce energy consumption, and enhance occupant comfort and safety. Smart buildings may also incorporate features like automated blinds, voice-activated controls, and indoor air quality monitoring to further improve the overall experience for occupants. The goal of a smart building is to create a more efficient, sustainable, and responsive environment for its occupants while minimizing the impact on the environment
